IDEA TITLE
INNOVATE-X-NIET

Proposed Solution

An AI-powered placement readiness platform that verifies student skills through staged assessments and real evidence, then creates personalized roadmaps, recruiter trust profiles, and college-level analytics.

Detailed Explanation

  • Students upload a resume or enter their skills, target role, and dream company.
  • A 3-stage verification assessment checks actual skill depth using easy, medium, and hard questions with rising time limits.
  • The trust model analyzes correctness, time usage, confidence behavior, project proof, and coding-profile evidence.
  • The system generates a dynamic roadmap with resources, assignments, mini-projects, and proof-based progression.
  • Resume/ATS feedback, gamified progress, and leaderboard mechanics keep students motivated and visible.
